Best Pancakes

Description

These are great pancakes. Why? Because I make them from scratch.

Ingredients

  1. Flour
  2. Milk
  3. Oil

  5. Baking Soda
  6. Cream of Tartar
  7. Sugar

Instructions

  • Mix together wet ingredients.
  • Mix in all dry ingredients except for the flour.
  • Slowly add in the flour, mixing as you go.
  • On a preheated pan, melt butter.
  • Add pancake batter to hot pan.
  • Flip ounce bubbly.
  • Finish ounce looking tasty.
  • Eat the yummy pancakes.
